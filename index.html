Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to some of the most common questions about Funngro.

Funngro is a platform that connects young talent with real companies for project-based work opportunities. By registering, completing a profile, and applying to projects, users can gain valuable experience, earn money, and receive certificates.

Download the Funngro app from the App Store or Google Play. After installation, register with your details, choose your area of interest, and complete your profile to start applying for projects.

Once you complete a project and it is approved by the company, your payment will be processed through Funngro's payment system. You can check your earnings in the app under your profile.

Funngro is open to young individuals interested in gaining work experience and earning income. You should have a completed profile with relevant skills or interests, and the motivation to complete project-based work for various companies.

For any assistance, you can contact Funngro support through the Help section in the app or by emailing support@funngro.com. Our support team is here to help you with any queries.
